Seven Wonders In The World

The Seven Wonders of the World are a group of places around the globe that are considered to be of great importance.
These are:The Colosseum in Italy, Petra in Jordan, Chichen Itza in Mexico, Christ the Redeemer in Brazil, Machu picchu in Peru, Taj Mahal in India and The Great Wall of China.
